# Claim Boundaries

Vault status: protected claim gate.

## Allowed Claims

Use these when source evidence supports them:

- VULCA is an agent-native visual control layer.
- VULCA is moving toward a Workspace / Creative Repo product model.
- VULCA has SDK, CLI, MCP, and skill surfaces.
- VULCA has implemented image decomposition, layer editing, redraw, inpaint,
  composite, pasteback, provider routing, and L1-L5 evaluation capabilities.
- VULCA records artifacts and case records for review and future learning.
- VULCA can structure creative work through briefs, motif branches, variants,
  evidence packs, and release gates.
- VULCA keeps unsupported public claims blocked through review boundaries.

## Claims Requiring Qualification

These must include status language such as `internal`, `draft`, `preview`,
`public blocked`, or `requires human review`:

- ChatGPT Prompt Studio
- provider-backed real image quality
- redraw quality
- target-aware mask refinement quality
- cultural evaluation
- PPT / deck generation
- Workspace product maturity
- website public positioning
- telemetry usage signals

## Disallowed Claims Without Strong Gate Evidence

Do not claim:

- VULCA replaces Figma, Canva, Adobe, Runway, FLORA, Krea, or other creative
  tools.
- VULCA guarantees cultural correctness.
- VULCA guarantees commercial safety, legal clearance, or indemnification.
- VULCA has solved presentation design.
- VULCA outputs are public-ready when the artifact says `public_blocked`.
- AI-assisted labels are human-confirmed.
- Automated L1-L5 scoring replaces expert or human review.
- Internal proof pipelines are customer-ready.
- Current PPT outputs are production-quality.
- Public Codex listing is live unless verified by the platform.

## Required Downgrade Language

When evidence is incomplete, use:

- implemented contract
- local test evidence
- internal proof
- preview-gated
- public blocked
- human review required
- claim boundary preserved
- source-backed synthesis

## Human Confirmation Rule

Only a human can confirm:

- final public-readiness
- production-quality approval
- legal or commercial safety
- cultural authority
- final adjudication labels
- release approval

AI or agent review can recommend, summarize, and flag. It cannot convert a
blocked or draft artifact into a final human-confirmed claim.
