# Commercial Brief

## Audience

Builders, designers, platform reviewers, and early technical adopters who understand presentation tools and AI agents but need to see why Vulca is different from prompt-only deck generation.

## Decision

The deck must make the audience believe that Vulca can convert real commercial design references into repeatable, editable presentation code, and that this is more defensible than generating a deck from one generic prompt.

## Business Context

This case pack supports the first Vulca PPT product wedge. The resulting launch deck can be used in a demo video, product page, investor conversation, and platform-review context.

## Design Need

The deck must feel commercially credible and design-led. It needs a clear claim spine, visible system logic, controlled use of visuals, and proof that output remains editable rather than flattened into images.

## Technical Complexity

Medium-high. The deck must explain source references, case packs, skills, code generation, SVG or bitmap asset rules, Gemini review, and layout QA without turning into an engineering architecture document.

## Emotional Tone

Precise, premium, controlled, builder-oriented, culturally aware, and skeptical of generic AI gloss.

## Call To Action

Run the Vulca PPT workflow on a real commercial deck need, compare it against a prompt-only baseline, and choose which slide primitives should become product features.
