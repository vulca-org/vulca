# Brand-Safe AI Creative Production Deck Brief

Status: draft-public-blocked

## Audience

AI product builders, brand operations leaders, creative workflow owners, and design-led founders evaluating whether AI creative production should be governed as a reusable workflow.

## Decision

Should a brand team use a governed AI creative workflow instead of uncontrolled one-shot generation?

## Thesis

The next AI creative product category is not "generate an image"; it is a governed workflow that turns approved brand inputs into reviewable variants across channels.

## Product Or Workflow Concept

A governed campaign workflow builder that turns approved briefs, approved assets, channel requirements, localization needs, and review rules into editable creative variants and a presentation-ready review packet.

Coca-Cola and Adobe are cited only as independent industry reference sources. The deck must not frame either company as a partner, customer, integration, sponsor, or endorsement.

## Failure Modes

- The deck implies Coca-Cola or Adobe endorses Vulca.
- The deck copies source visuals, screenshots, logos, campaign layouts, or long source prose.
- The deck invents adoption numbers, revenue, performance, speed, or customer outcomes.
- The deck becomes a generic AI image-generation pitch instead of a workflow product deck.
- The deck exposes internal implementation vocabulary in audience-facing copy.
- The deck implies governance can replace human review or guarantee brand safety.
