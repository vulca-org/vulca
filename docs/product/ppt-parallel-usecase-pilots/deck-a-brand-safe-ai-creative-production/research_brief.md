## Forbidden Or Unproven Claims

| Claim | Reason |
| --- | --- |
| Coca-Cola or Adobe endorses Vulca. | Unsupported by the sources and explicitly out of bounds. |
| Vulca uses or is allowed to use Coca-Cola or Adobe assets, logos, screenshots, or layouts. | Unsupported and forbidden by the deck boundaries. |
| Vulca has produced adoption, revenue, cost savings, launch-speed, or performance results for brand teams. | No source provided for Vulca outcomes or quantified customer results. |
| Governed AI production guarantees brand safety or compliance. | Sources support controls, validation, approvals, and governance, not guarantees. |
| The product fully replaces human creative review. | Adobe sources describe review, approvals, flagged items, and human-in-the-loop patterns. |
| Vulca is equivalent to, better than, or integrated with Adobe Firefly Creative Production. | Unsupported and unnecessary for the deck goal. |
