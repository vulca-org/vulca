# VULCA Blind Evaluation — Scoring Rubric

## Likert Scale Anchors (1-5)

### cultural_fit (E1: Image)

| Score | Anchor |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | No cultural relevance; random or inappropriate imagery |
| 2 | Minimal cultural cues; largely generic or superficial |
| 3 | Some cultural elements present but incomplete or inaccurate |
| 4 | Clear cultural representation with minor gaps |
| 5 | Excellent cultural fidelity; authentic motifs, composition, and style |

### evidence_chain (E2: Critique)

| Score | Anchor |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | No specific visual evidence cited; purely subjective opinion |
| 2 | Vague references ("the colors are nice") without grounding |
| 3 | Some concrete references to visual elements but chain is incomplete |
| 4 | Mostly grounded; cites composition, brushwork, or color with reasoning |
| 5 | Fully grounded; every claim supported by specific visual evidence |

### cross_cultural (E2: Critique)

| Score | Anchor |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | No cultural context; treats artwork in isolation |
| 2 | Mentions culture but superficially or with stereotypes |
| 3 | Shows awareness of the tradition but limited comparative depth |
| 4 | Meaningful cross-cultural comparison with relevant context |
| 5 | Deep cross-cultural insight; nuanced understanding of multiple traditions |

### self_consistency (E2: Critique)

| Score | Anchor |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Contradictory statements; conclusion does not follow from analysis |
| 2 | Major logical gaps or unsupported leaps |
| 3 | Generally coherent but with minor inconsistencies |
| 4 | Logically sound; premises support conclusion with minor issues |
| 5 | Perfectly consistent; clear logical flow from evidence to conclusion |
